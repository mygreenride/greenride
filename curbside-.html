2 Phone Call Policy

1st Call

  • Call us as soon as your plane lands.
  • Toll-Free: 800-893-7210 / Call: 619-200-2060 before you exit the plane.

2nd Call

  • Call again when you are outside at the main exit by the curb.
  • Find the nearest zone sign (A, B, C, etc.) and tell us your zone.

After your 2nd call, your driver will pull up to you.

Find the Nearest Zone Sign

Right at the curb — look up for the lettered sign and have it ready when you call.
