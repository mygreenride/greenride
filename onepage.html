Credit Card Authorization & Payment Policy

New customers: The physical credit card and cardholder must be present at pickup. We do not accept remote, phone, or emailed card authorizations for first-time customers; please pay with cash or present the card in person.

Returning customers: Remote credit card authorization is available for repeat customers in good standing; additional verification may be required.

These measures protect our customers and drivers and help prevent unauthorized card use.

Cancellation Policy

Email only. Send cancellations from the same email used on your reservation (or the credit-card authorization email).

Email: Cancellation@GreenRide.net

  • Full refund if emailed at least 4 hours in advance within business hours prior to pickup.
  • No refunds for less than 4 hours’ notice or no-shows.
  • Refunds typically appear within 2 business days (varies by card/bank).
  • Phone calls are not accepted for cancellations.

Airport Pickup Notes

  • Meet & Greet: Driver meets at baggage claim. If you can’t find your driver, call 800-893-7210.
  • Curbside (SAN): Make two calls—(1) when phone use is permitted on the plane; (2) when you are curbside with bags to dispatch the driver.
