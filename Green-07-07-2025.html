San Diego Information

San Diego is California's second largest city and the United States' eighth largest, encompassing charming neighborhoods like the Gaslamp Quarter, Little Italy, Coronado, La Jolla, Del Mar, Carlsbad, Escondido, La Mesa, Hillcrest, Barrio Logan, and Chula Vista.

San Diego is renowned for its idyllic climate, 70 miles of pristine beaches, and world-class attractions including the San Diego Zoo, Wild Animal Park, SeaWorld, LEGOLAND, and Balboa Park.

For more information, visit the San Diego Convention and Visitors Bureau.

San Diego International Airport (SAN) Information

Founded in 1928, San Diego International Airport is the 30th busiest airport in the country and the busiest single-runway commercial service airport in the nation.

Located three miles northwest of downtown San Diego, the airport operates close to 300 departures daily and contributes an estimated $9.9 billion annually to the regional economy.
