How to Cancel (Email Only)

Phone calls are not accepted for cancellations.

Send an email from the same email address used on your online reservation (or the credit-card authorization fax email) to:

Cancellation@GreenRide.net

Include: Date, Time, Pick-up address, Drop-off address, Passenger phone number, Reason for cancellation.

Refunds & Timing

  • Full refund when you email your cancellation at least 4 hours in advance within our business hours prior to pickup.
  • Refunds typically appear within 2 business days (varies by card and issuing bank).
  • No refunds for cancellations with less than 4 hours’ email notice (within business hours) or for no-shows.

Rates (Online Reservations)

All online reservation rates must match the price listed on the Airport Rates page. It is the customer’s responsibility to confirm the correct rate on the website.

If a customer provides a rate that differs from the website, it will be treated as a wish rate rather than the actual rate. By submitting a reservation, you agree to all GreenRide.net terms and conditions.
